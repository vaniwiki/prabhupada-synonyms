==== kamala-nābhāya  ====
  * unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, from whose abdomen the original lotus flower originated --- [[books:sb:4:30:25|SB 4.30.25]]

==== paṅkaja-nābhāya  ====
  * unto the Lord who has a specific depression resembling a lotus flower in the center of His abdomen --- [[books:sb:1:8:22|SB 1.8.22]]
  * unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, from whose navel the lotus flower emanates --- [[books:sb:4:24:34|SB 4.24.34]]

==== tri-nābhāya  ====
  * within the stem growing from whose navel live all the three worlds --- [[books:sb:8:17:26|SB 8.17.26]]
