==== deha-upādhi brahma  ====
  * Brahman having the body as a designation --- [[books:cc:madhya:24:212|Madhya 24.212]]

==== sarva-upādhi-vinirmuktam  ====
  * free from all kinds of material designations, or free from all desires except the desire to render service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead --- [[books:cc:madhya:19:170|Madhya 19.170]]
