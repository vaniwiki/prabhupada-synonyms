==== chanda-anuvartinaḥ  ====
  * who are followers of the whims of death --- [[books:sb:8:16:4|SB 8.16.4]]

==== dharma-anuvartinaḥ  ====
  * those who are pious and act according to the regulative principles or Vedic injunctions --- [[books:sb:5:26:37|SB 5.26.37]]

==== rantideva-anuvartinaḥ  ====
  * the followers of King Rantideva (that is, his servants, his family members, his friends and others) --- [[books:sb:9:21:18|SB 9.21.18]]
